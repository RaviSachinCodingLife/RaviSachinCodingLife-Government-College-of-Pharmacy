About Institute

Government College of Pharmacy, Aurangabad was established in 1996 through the efforts of Honorable Late Shri. Govindbhaiji Shroff as part of his social obligation — fulfilling a long-standing need for the Marathwada region.

Now housed in a modern 8000 sq. ft. facility, the institute has quickly established a reputation for providing quality education. Affiliated with Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad and approved by AICTE and the Government of Maharashtra, the college offers:

- Four-year B.Pharm. program with an annual intake of 60 students
- Two-year M.Pharm (Pharmaceutics) with 18 seats
- A six-year integrated course
- Facilities for doctoral studies through the university

The college continues to grow as a centre of academic excellence in pharmaceutical education and research.

About

I am honored to welcome you to Government College of Pharmacy, Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar — a venture of the Government of Maharashtra, established in 1996 with a futuristic vision to provide qualitative education using innovative and dynamic pedagogical techniques.

Since inception, the institute has focused on delivering health care services with dedication and commitment. Our vision and mission are reflected in our faculty, staff, students, and alumni.

We strongly believe that exposure is the key in shaping personalities, and our team continuously strives to transform students into professionals capable of meeting global challenges. The serene campus, cutting-edge infrastructure, and research opportunities make it a hub for academic excellence.

We are proud to see our students consistently achieving excellence and our alumni holding esteemed positions worldwide.
