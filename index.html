About Institute

Government College of Pharmacy, Aurangabad was established in 1996 through the efforts of Honorable Late Shri. Govindbhaiji Shroff as part of his social obligation — fulfilling a long-standing need for the Marathwada region. Initially functioning as a department within the Government College of Engineering, it gained status as an independent Government College in 1999.

Now housed in a modern 8000 sq. ft. facility, the institute has quickly established a reputation for providing quality education. Affiliated with Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad and approved by AICTE and the Government of Maharashtra, the college offers:

- Four-year B.Pharm. program with an annual intake of 60 students
- Two-year M.Pharm (Pharmaceutics) with 18 seats
- A six-year integrated course
- Facilities for doctoral studies through the university

The college continues to grow as a centre of academic excellence in pharmaceutical education and research.
