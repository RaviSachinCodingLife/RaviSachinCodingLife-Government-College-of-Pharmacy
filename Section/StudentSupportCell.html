Student Support Cells

The institute has various student support cells to ensure a safe, inclusive and supportive campus environment.

Active

Anti Ragging Cell

Ensures a ragging-free campus, spreads awareness and addresses any complaints related to ragging.

  • Awareness programs and campaigns
  • Confidential reporting mechanism
  • Immediate action on complaints
View Anti Ragging Report 2025
Well-being

Counselling & Mentoring Cell

Provides academic and personal counselling support to students and helps them cope with stress and challenges.

  • One-to-one counselling sessions
  • Mentor-mentee guidance
  • Workshops on mental well-being
View Guidelines
Support

Student Grievance Redressal Cell

Addresses grievances related to academics, administration and other student issues in a fair and transparent manner.

  • Online and offline grievance submission
  • Time-bound resolution
  • Confidential handling of complaints
Submit Grievance / View Format
Safety

Women Development & ICC

Promotes gender sensitization and ensures a safe and respectful environment for girl students and women staff.

  • Awareness sessions and campaigns
  • Support for cases of harassment
  • Strict confidentiality and action
View Policy / Committee
Inclusion

SC / ST / OBC / Minority Cell

Facilitates scholarships, reservations and support services for students from reserved and minority categories.

  • Information on government schemes
  • Assistance in documentation
  • Guidance for competitive exams
View Notices / Circulars
Engagement

NSS & Extension Activities

Encourages students to participate in community service, outreach programs and extension activities.

  • Community health camps
  • Social awareness drives
  • Student leadership opportunities
