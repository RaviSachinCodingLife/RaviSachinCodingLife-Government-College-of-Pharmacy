Alumni Association

The association is formed with an ambition of striving for the success of its alumni on the national and international fronts. A wide range of activities are planned to bring the alumni onto a single platform regularly and provide the outgoing students a glimpse of the outer world including the varieties of jobs to be taken up.

Aims and Objectives

  • Encourage the spirit of brotherhood among the Alumni.
  • Provide a forum to the Alumni for exchange of ideas on academic, cultural and social issues of the day.
  • Look after the general interests of the Alumni.
  • Bring out publications for the fulfillment of the objectives of the Association.
  • Arrange social and cultural functions.
  • Provide better knowledge regarding the profession.
  • Raise endowment funds and award stipends to deserving students on a need-cum-merit basis; assist alumni in need.
  • Facilitate campus interviews and placement opportunities for fresh graduates.
