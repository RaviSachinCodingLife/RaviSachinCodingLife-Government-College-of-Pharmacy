Eligibility Criteria

B.Pharm

Candidates must have passed HSC or its equivalent examination with Physics and Chemistry as compulsory subjects along with Mathematics or Biology, securing at least 45% marks (40% for Backward Class, EWS, and PwD candidates from Maharashtra). Must obtain a non-zero score in CET conducted by the Competent Authority.

Lateral Entry (Direct Second Year B.Pharm)

Candidates must have passed a Diploma in Pharmacy with at least 45% marks (40% for reserved categories, EWS, and PwD candidates from Maharashtra) from a PCI, Central, or State Government approved institution or equivalent.

Pharm D

Candidates must have passed HSC or its equivalent with Physics and Chemistry as compulsory subjects along with Mathematics or Biology, securing at least 50% marks (45% for reserved categories, EWS, and PwD candidates from Maharashtra) or a Diploma in Pharmacy from a recognized institution with similar minimum marks. Must obtain a non-zero score in CET conducted by the Competent Authority.
