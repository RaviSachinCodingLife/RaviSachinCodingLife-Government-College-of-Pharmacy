About Us

Government College of Pharmacy, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar has been started in 1996 with the efforts of Honorable Late Shri. Govindbhaiji Shroff as a part of his social obligation to which Marathwada region was awaiting. Initially, it has been started as one of the Departments of Government College of Engineering, Chatrapati Sambhajinagar. Subsequently in 1999, it acquired the status of full-fledged Government College. Now, the college is run in its own building of around 8000 sq. ft. The institute in its short span has established credibility to impart quality education to masses. The institute is affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Chatrapati Sambhajinagar and is approved by PCI and Government of Maharashtra. It is conducting four years B.Pharm course with annual intake of 60 students, two year M.Pharm (Pharmaceutics: intake - 15) and M.Pharm (Pharmaceutical Chemistry: intake - 08) and 6 years Post Baccalaureate integrated Pharm D course. The college is also an approved research centre for doctoral studies from Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada Univeristy, Chatrapati Sambhajinagar.
