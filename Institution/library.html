Central Library, Govt. College of Pharmacy

Library

The Central Library of the institute serves as a stack stock of over 70,000 volumes and includes textbooks, reference books, handbooks, and other learning materials. The Govt. College of Pharmacy Library has institutional membership in the DELNET network and subscribes to many online journals and databases for research and education.

In addition to printed resources, the library offers access to various e-resources like NPTEL, Swayam, J-Gate, and N-List. It has a reading room with comfortable seating, internet access, and a peaceful environment conducive to learning.

The Library operates under a semi-automated system and is staffed by trained professionals.

E-RESOURCE SECTION

All the resources in the library (Books and Journals) are accessible through:

  • Online Public Access Catalog (OPAC)
  • DELNET
  • Membership of IIC, SCIENCE DIRECT
  • J-GATE
  • N-LIST
  • AICTE Resources
  • Remote Access Facility
  • Online & Print Journals

The total collection of e-journals in the E-Resource Section includes:

  • Total number of titles: 1562
  • Total number of volumes: 8686
  • Printed Journals: 19
  • E-Journals: J-Gate, N-List
  • NPTEL, Swayam Access
E-RESOURCE SECTION

Full-text access of E-Resources listed below is available in the library for students and faculty members:

  • NPTEL
  • SWAYAM
  • J-GATE
  • N-LIST
  • DELNET

These journals help students and staff stay updated with the latest research across multiple disciplines.

SPECIAL FEATURES OF CENTRAL LIBRARY
  • Supports access by every department
  • Separate issue counter for each section
  • Online access to OPAC
  • Membership of DELNET
  • Easy access to E-Resources (J-Gate, N-LIST)
  • Facility for research scholars
  • Reading rooms with Wi-Fi
Special Features of Central Library

The Central Library of Govt. College of Pharmacy is designed to support the academic and research needs of B.Pharm. students and faculty members.

  • Spacious reading environment
  • Latest edition of textbooks and journals
  • Integrated access to digital and physical content
  • Reference and lending section with user-friendly services
  • Institutional membership of N-LIST and DELNET
